Online Curriculum Print Flyer

The biggest challenge I faced designing this flyer was creating an information hierarchy. All the copy is important and needed to be easily digestible. I started my process by drawing 8 thumbnails for each page of the flyer. Working from the best elements of each set of thumbnails, I sketched out a larger and more detailed design. This exercise allowed me to move to digital creation where the final design quickly came together.
